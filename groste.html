Grostè Ski Area

Discover a high-altitude ski area known for wide open views, excellent snow conditions and a dynamic alpine landscape.

About This Area

Grostè is one of the highest and most panoramic ski areas in Madonna di Campiglio. It is appreciated for its altitude, snow quality and impressive mountain scenery. The area offers a strong sporting identity while also providing access to lifts, mountain huts and enjoyable stops throughout the day.
