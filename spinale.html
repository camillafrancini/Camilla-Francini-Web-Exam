Spinale Ski Area

Discover slopes, lifts and mountain huts in one of the most scenic parts of Madonna di Campiglio.

About This Area

Spinale is one of the main ski areas of Madonna di Campiglio. It offers panoramic views, well-known slopes, modern lift facilities and several mountain huts where visitors can relax during the day.
