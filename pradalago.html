Pradalago Ski Area

Explore slopes, lifts and mountain huts in one of the most accessible and family-friendly areas of Madonna di Campiglio.

About This Area

Pradalago is one of the most popular ski areas in Madonna di Campiglio. It is known for its comfortable lift system, enjoyable slopes and welcoming alpine atmosphere. It is a great area for skiers looking for a balanced combination of sport, relaxation and scenic beauty.
